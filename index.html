MT15
Yamaha MT 15 is a neked version of the Yamaha R15 V3 and its design is inspired by the MT09.The MT 15 comes with 155 cc single cylinder liquid cooled SOHC engine which produces the max power of 18.5 PS at 10000 rpm and 13.9 Nm torque at 8500 rpm.The bike comes with a 6 speed gearbox.
